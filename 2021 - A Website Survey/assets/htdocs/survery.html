DIRECTIONS

This is a simple survey taking information about what people like most in websites i.e. what makes a website look good for them. There are only 2 parts which include color and fonts.

You may need to zoom in and out at first to make all the questions fit in the card depending on your browser. Please answer all the question in the survey card and when read click next.

At the end of the survey you will be able to see a summary of all of your previous choices. Thats all and have fun!



Notes for the graphs page at the end of the survey:
   1. You may need to answer the survey several times for the graphs to look useful.
   2. The sections in the graphs with averages also average the default values.
   3. If anything seems out of place try resizing the browser window a bit.
